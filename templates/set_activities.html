Activities

The most successful launderettes are those who offer more than just a self-service facility for washing & drying.

  • Some of these additional services include:
  • Duvet Cleaning Service.
  • Drop Off & Pick-Up Service.
  • Shirt Laundry.
  • Local Contracts i.e Hair Salons / Hotels / Restaurants / etc.
  • Ironing Service (This is very popular with most income couples).
